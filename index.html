About

I am a 24-year-old WordPress web-designer at NV Technologies based in Johannesburg. I was born in 1998 and spent most of childhood in the East-Rand area of Gauteng.
From a young age I had an interest in modern technology which inspired me to go into the direction I am today, and I am extremely excited to start building on the knowledge I have obtained over the years.

The tech industry has always been a passion of mine and from a young age I was very interested in computers and technology. I would often take things apart, and re-build them, or try to replicate how something works. I enjoy adventurous outdoor activities, and find my happy place in the outdoors, but I also enjoy staying home and spending time with my loved ones.

My main goals in the future are building a successful career, having a loving relationship with my loved ones, and creating memories by doing the things I love to do.
